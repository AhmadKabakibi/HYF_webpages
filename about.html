Want to join us?

The current job market is in high need of software developers. At the same time we have a lot of talented refugees entering Europe who often struggle finding work in their original field of study. We want to help this talent find a career where they can fulfil their potential and become participating citizens in their new country of residence.

What do we do?

The core language we teach is JavaScript, and we teach our students HTML, CSS, Node, Angular, Express and MongoDB. We teach our students how to think like a programmer, how to solve problems independently, and how to manage a project.

With this fundamental knowledge our students should be ready for their first software-developer job from which they will start learning on the job. We regularly organise company visits and connect our students to hiring companies when they graduate.
